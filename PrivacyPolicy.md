# Privacy Policy for LottoLedger

**Effective Date:** December 17, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0

## Overview

LottoLedger is a privacy-first iOS lottery companion app designed to help users generate lottery tickets using structured randomness, track drawings, compare results, and analyze performance history. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

**The simple truth: LottoLedger collects ZERO data. Everything stays on your device.**

## What LottoLedger Does

LottoLedger helps you:
- Generate lottery tickets with various distribution strategies
- Store and manage tickets locally
- Record drawing results
- Compare tickets to drawings automatically
- View statistics and performance reports
- Understand lottery odds and probabilities

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device only.**

LottoLedger stores the following information **exclusively on your device** in the app's private Documents directory:

- **Tickets**: Lottery tickets you generate (stored in `tickets.json`)
- **Drawings**: Drawing results you manually enter (stored in `drawings.json`)
- **Matches**: Results from comparing your tickets to drawings (stored in `matches.json`)
- **Game Configurations**: Your custom lottery game settings (stored in `games.json`)

This data:
- ‚úÖ Remains entirely on your device
- ‚úÖ Is never transmitted anywhere
